About CYGNUS'25

CYGNUS is a National level Symposium that is conducted every year by the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. This symposium serves as a platform for discussing upcoming trends, exploring the latest technological advancements, and sharing innovative research. Imagine a world where technical prowess meets boundless fun. Here are 8 events spanning various domains that infuse technology with excitement and joy. All these events will feature guest speakers and experts who will share their knowledge and insights with the attendees. This symposium plays a crucial role in bridging the academic and industrial gap which contributes to the advancement of innovation and technology in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.

General Guidelines

Event Participation Rules

Registration Requirements

All participants must register beforehand through the online registration portal. On-spot registrations will be subject to availability and additional fees.

Event Pricing

All events excluding Watt A Biryani - ₹50/-
All events Including Watt A Biryani - ₹200/-

Team Formation

Teams should consist of 2-4 members for group events. Individual participants are welcome for solo events.

Dress Code

Participants are expected to dress in smart casuals. College ID cards are mandatory for all participants.

Judging Criteria

All events will be judged based on innovation, technical accuracy, presentation skills, and practical implementation. The decision of judges will be final.

Code of Conduct

Participants are expected to maintain the decorum of the institution. Any misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification.

Certificates

Participation certificates will be provided to all registered participants. Winners will receive additional merit certificates and prizes.

Punctuality

Participants must report 30 minutes before their event.

Electronics

Mobile phones must be silenced during all events.

Equipment

Bring your own laptops for coding and design events.
